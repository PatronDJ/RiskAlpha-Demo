Build vs buy decision
Buy — loan system of record
Loanworks (AU-native) or SAMS for structured finance. These handle operational loan management — drawdowns, repayments, facility amendments. They become the source that feeds the warehouse.
Build — calculation engine
Python (pandas/numpy/scipy) + dbt + Snowflake. Every calculation is version-controlled in Git, testable with pytest, and auditable. Enterprise loan systems (Loan IQ, nCino) are bank-grade overkill at $600M.
